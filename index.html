Why I Do
What I Do

I had access to education that many around me did not, and that shaped how I see responsibility.

Over time, I realized that education is not only about access — it is about quality, opportunity, and the ability to apply knowledge meaningfully.

This realization led me to combine software development, public speaking, and youth engagement as tools for impact.

I believe young people should not be excluded from the systems that shape their future — they should help build them.
